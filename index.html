Our Product

Rice Husk Charcoal

The staple food of Indonesians is rice, so Indonesia always produces a lot of rice husks. We burn it into rice husk charcoal in the traditional way while maintaining quality. We dry the rice husks first so that the combustion process is maximized. So you can get the best rice husk charcoal at an affordable price.

Rice Husk Ash

Rice husk ash is an abundantly available and renewable agriculture by product from rice milling in the rice producing countries. It has the highest proportion of silica content among all plant residues. A rice mill turns the paddy plant into 78% rice, 20% rice husk and 2% is lost in the process.
